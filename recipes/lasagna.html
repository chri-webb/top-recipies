Webbs Famous Lasagna

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500g (about 1 lb) ground beef or a mix of beef and pork
  • 1 x 800g (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 x 400g (14 oz) can tomato passata or pureed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil (or a few fresh leaves)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 12-15 lasagna noodles (oven-ready or regular, pre-boiled if necessary)
  • 500g (about 1 lb) ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, plus more for topping
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, plus more for topping
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish (optional)

For the Béchamel Sauce:

  • 50g (3.5 tbsp) unsalted butter
  • 50g (1/2 cup) all-purpose flour
  • 600ml (2.5 cups) warm milk
  • Pinch of nutmeg
  • Salt and white pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    1. Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5-7 minutes.
    2. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute more until fragrant.
    3. Add ground beef (or mix) and cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until browned. Drain any excess fat.
    4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato passata, tomato paste, oregano, and basil. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for at least 30-45 minutes (the longer, the better for flavor development), stirring occasionally. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Prepare the Béchamel Sauce:
    1. In a separate saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
    2. Whisk in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly, to form a roux.
    3. Gradually whisk in the warm milk, a little at a time, making sure to whisk out any lumps. Continue whisking until the sauce thickens and is smooth.
    4. Season with nutmeg, salt, and white pepper. Keep warm.
  3. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
    1. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. Mix well.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna:
    1. Preheat your oven to 190°C (375°F). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch (23x33 cm) baking dish.
    2. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
    3. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce (break them if needed to fit). If using regular noodles, ensure they are pre-boiled according to package directions.
    4. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture evenly over the noodles.
    5. Spoon about a third of the béchamel sauce over the ricotta.
    6. Sprinkle with a generous layer of mozzarella cheese.
    7. Add another layer of meat sauce.
    8. Repeat the layers: noodles, remaining ricotta, béchamel, mozzarella, meat sauce.
    9. Top with a final layer of noodles, the remaining béchamel sauce, and a generous sprinkling of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake the Lasagna:
    1. Cover the baking dish loosely with aluminum foil (you might want to spray the underside of the foil with cooking spray to prevent cheese from sticking).
    2. Bake for 25 minutes.
    3. Remove the foil and bake for another 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and bubbly.
  6. Rest and Serve:
    1. Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. This helps the layers set and makes for easier serving.
    2. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley, if desired.

Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
