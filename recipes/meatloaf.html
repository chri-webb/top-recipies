Pretty Decent Meatloaf

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1.5 lbs (about 680g) ground beef (80/20 lean preferred)
  • 1/2 cup plain breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tsp dried thyme (or 1 tbsp fresh)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

For the Glaze:

  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar (packed)
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar (or white vinegar)
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Aromatics:
    1. Heat olive oil in a small pan over medium heat. Add finely chopped onion and cook until softened and translucent, about 5-7 minutes.
    2. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  2. Combine Meatloaf Ingredients:
    1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, cooled cooked onion and garlic, breadcrumbs, milk, beaten egg, chopped parsley, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper.
    2. Using your hands (or a spoon), gently mix the ingredients until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can make the meatloaf tough.
  3. Form the Meatloaf:
    1. Lightly grease a baking sheet or a 9x5 inch (23x13 cm) loaf pan. If using a baking sheet, form the meat mixture into a loaf shape, approximately 9 inches long and 4-5 inches wide. If using a loaf pan, press the mixture evenly into the pan.
  4. Prepare the Glaze:
    1. In a small bowl, whisk together the ketchup, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, and Dijon mustard until well combined.
  5. Bake the Meatloaf:
    1. Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F).
    2. Spread about half of the glaze evenly over the top and sides of the meatloaf.
    3. Bake for 45 minutes.
    4. Remove the meatloaf from the oven and spread the remaining glaze over the top.
    5. Return to the oven and continue baking for another 15-25 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 71°C (160°F) for beef. (If using a mix with pork, ensure it reaches 71°C/160°F).
  6. Rest and Serve:
    1. Once cooked, remove the meatloaf from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing. This allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in a moister meatloaf.
    2. Slice and serve warm with your favorite side dishes, such as mashed potatoes or green beans.

Enjoy your classic homestyle meatloaf!
